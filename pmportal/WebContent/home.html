About PMPortal

PM-Portal is a project management tool initially developed by interns working for SGT Inc. The tool pulls together project information from other project management tools such as Jira or Bugzilla and displays a project's status or other metrics. The tools uses the agile scrum methodology, dividing various work phases into sprints which seek to satisfy user story requirements. As of 7/29/16, Jira is the only tool supported.

The Release page of the dashboard gives you information on individual projects suchs as due date, projected due date, issues completed and SEA & EEA data.

The Metrics page of the dashboard gives you more detailed metrics about a project as well as a look at metrics spanning across all projects

The Portal will give you information about projects that are not doing as well as planned as well as give you insight on your planning methods. The Reports page is a great place to go to see straight on how you can improve your project managing.
